Our company offers a wide range of services to individuals and business clients. We provide our clients with personalized services at a reasonable cost. Below is a list of services that we offer to our clients.

Federal & State Tax Preparation For Individuals:

We prepare federal and state returns for individuals. This includes long and short forms, the form we use depends on your personal tax situation. We are well versed in the IRS tax laws, therefore, we will ensure you don't miss any deductions.

Sole Proprietorship Preparation:

Are you a Sole Proprietor, in network marketing (Avon, Mary Kay, etc) or a small business owner? We prepare tax returns for these business entities as well.

Partnership Tax Preparation:

Have a partnership and need to have your taxes filed? We prepare returns for multiple-member partnerships.

Electronic Filing (e-file):

We provide electronic filing at no additional cost to our clients. Electronic filing is not only safe and secure, but is fast. You have the option of having a check mailed to you by the IRS (Internal Revenue Service) or have the funds deposited into your checking or savings account. This method of filing is strongly encouraged by the IRS.

Tax Returns By Mail:

Mail your tax information to us and we can provide you a preparation fee estimate, prepare your tax return and mail the federal, state, and client copies to your address.

Tax Returns By Fax:

Fax your tax information to us and we can provide you a preparation fee estimate, prepare your tax return and mail the federal, state, and client copies to your address.

Tax Return By Email:

Email your tax information to us and we can provide you a preparation fee estimate, prepare your tax return and mail the federal, state, and client copies to your address

Tax Planning:

There is a popular thought process regarding tax planning and tax cutting. Many people feel that cutting taxes and sheltering income are just for the wealthy. However, the tax law is the same for everyone. No American is required to pay more taxes than the law demands. We provide tax planning services for our clients which reduce their tax liabilities.

Bookkeeping:

For many small and medium sized businesses, having an accounting/bookkeeping department is not consistent with their goals, objectives or needs. Camfinancials provides bookkeeping services, which allows you to focus on what you do best, run your business.
